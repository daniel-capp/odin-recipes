Lasagna

How to make an authentic Italian lasagna

Straight from the streets of Bologna, Italy!

Ingredients:

  • Pureed tomatoes
  • Ground veal
  • Onion - chopped
  • Garlic - minced
  • Pasta sheets
  • Mozarella cheese - shredded

Steps:

  1. Make tomato sauce with ground veal, onion and garlic
  2. Spread tomatoes on pan
  3. Add pasta sheets
  4. Add cheese
  5. Keep repeating for several layers until pan is full. Should be at least 5 layers
